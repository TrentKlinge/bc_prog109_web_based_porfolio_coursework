Geol 107 Geologic Catastrophes taught by professor Gwyneth Jones

What I knew before I took this class

Before I took this course I knew a little bit about the difference between P waves and S waves which are different types of seismic waves and how earthquakes were triggered. I learned most of this in my sophomore chemestry class.

What I learned in this class

I learned a lot about some concepts that I was already familiar with and some concepts that I had never heard of. Some of the topics discussed in the class were emergency preparedness, earth cycles, earthquakes, tsunamis, landslides, floods, and global climate change. For most topics I learned about the processes that cause disasters, methods of minimizing the risk, and the effects and hazards of the disasters.
